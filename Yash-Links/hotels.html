Discover Dubai's best new hotels and upcoming hotel launches in 2024

FRI 31 MAY 2024

12 MIN READ

From boutique gems to luxury hotels, there are so many exciting addresses in which you can stay and play across the city. Check into these incredible new and upcoming hotels in Dubai.

Now Open

Check into the city's latest and greatest stays at any time

FIVE LUXE

Laidback elegance and all-out entertainment come together at FIVE LUXE in Jumeirah Beach Residence. This marks the third FIVE Hotels and Resorts property in Dubai and follows the brand’s famously fun-filled ethos – with plenty to excite and delight every visitor. The city's festive crowd can pick one of the hotel's 222 rooms and suites – some with features such as a DJ booth and dancefloor, plus an extra-large pool, spa and hot tubs – or move into one of the 276 residential apartments. For a memorable splurge during your stay, discover the exemplary dining scene at Japanese eatery Ronin, Mediterranean restaurant Cielo or French hotspot Tête A Tête. Or for glamorous Ibiza vibes, head to Playa Pacha, the hotel’s private beach and pool club that welcomes a regular lineup of international DJs to keep the party going.

Hotel : FIVE LUXE Where : Jumeirah Beach Residence Facilities : A total of 222 rooms and suites and 276 residential apartments, rooms with extra large private pools, beach club, spa, seven multi-cuisine dining and entertainment venues

Hilton Dubai Creek Hotel & Residences

A brand new property on the shores of Dubai Creek, Hilton Dubai Creek Hotel & Residences is the first opening within the upcoming 'Jewel of the Creek' development. With 180 rooms and suites – including studio, one-, two- and three-bedroom duplex penthouses – this hotel offers breathtaking vistas of both new and old Dubai. A fully-equipped fitness centre with a steam, sauna and indoor jacuzzi, an outdoor pool for adults, a separate pool for kids and an all-day dining restaurant make up the amenities of this new hotel in Dubai.

Hotel : Hilton Dubai Creek Hotel & Residences Where : Deira Facilities : 180 rooms and suites, views of old and new Dubai, fitness centre and dedicated swimming pools for adults and kids

Hilton Dubai Creek Hotel & Residences

Deira, Dubai

The Lana, Dorchester Collection, Dubai

Another of the city’s latest architectural marvels, The Lana stands airy and graceful over Marasi Bay Marina, with an unparalleled view of the Burj Khalifa. Highlights here include sky-high dining concepts, four of which have been created by chefs who have won multiple Michelin stars, and a rooftop pool and lounge from where to watch the busy urban precincts transform at sunset. The hotel has a selection of 225 elegant rooms and suites and will also be home to the first Dior Spa in the UAE, set to open in April 2024. Those planning on long-term stays ought to set their sights on the residences instead, which offer access to landscaped terraces, infinity pools, a yoga studio, a marina with 20 berths and a dedicated podium for retail and dining.

Hotel : The Lana Where : Business Bay Facilities : 225 rooms and suites, first Dior Spa in the UAE to open in April 2024, infinity pool, yoga studio and rooftop dining concepts

The Lana, Dorchester Collection

The Biltmore Hotel Villas

With a great location in the vicinity of Mall of the Emirates, The Biltmore Hotel Villas in Dubai offers the same opulence and hospitality as its 100-year-old Los Angeles counterpart. Choose from 18 three- or four-bedroom villas perfect for families and groups. Plus, there are two five-bedroom stays with a spacious rooftop lounge for gatherings. The interiors boast Emirati elegance coupled with a modern European design. Each villa features a private pool, jacuzzi, cinema room and smart home technology among other luxuries.

Hotel : The Biltmore Hotel Villas Where : Al Barsha 1 Facilities : 20 villas, private pools, cinema room and jacuzzi

The Biltmore Hotel Villas

HOTELS & ACCOMMODATION

Al Barsha 1 - Dubai/p>

One Za’abeel

Boasting the world’s longest cantilever – The Link – between its two sleek towers, One Za’abeel is an icon in the Dubai skyline. A stunning new addition to the city centre, the vertical resort goes beyond offering sophisticated stays with its ultra-chic retail, entertainment and culinary experiences. A spectacular rooftop offers the UAE’s longest infinity pool and views over Dubai’s charming old and dynamic new spaces. Keeping in theme with the word ‘Za’abeel’ (meaning ‘white sand’ in the local dialect), the rooms are elegantly dressed in neutral colour palettes and feature soothing sand motifs.

Hotel : One Za’abeel Where : Trade Centre Facilities : 229 rooms, eight restaurants inside The Link and three at The Garden. UAE’s longest suspended infinity pool
