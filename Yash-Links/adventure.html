Hatta Wadi

ADVENTURE

Hatta Wadi Hub

Put a pause on city life and embrace an outdoor adventure

9 REVIEWS

Take a short drive to the mountain town of Hatta for an incredible range of adventures. The rugged exclave is the perfect escape for thrill-seekers thanks to Hatta Wadi Hub, an activity centre with something to suit every adventurer, both big and small.

Rent a mountain bike and tackle 52km of trails, colour-coded according to difficulty, or hike Hatta’s rolling peaks at your own pace. Visitors to Hatta can also book alternative thrills such as archery or axe-throwing, or try zorbing nearby where you’ll roll downhill inside a giant transparent ball. There's even a huge 12m-high water slide where you can splash down, cool off and practise your flips.

Get into action at Hatta Wadi Hub

Want to catch a good view during your daring escapades? Add the Aerial Adventure Park to your checklist, if so. The park offers high-vantage activities, including a giant swing, a 45-degree zip line, a bag drop, a free fall zone and high ropes courses. While you fuel up on adrenaline, the kids can test out their intrepid inklings too at a specially designed set-up for children aged three to six.

For more unforgettable panoramas, make your way to the nearby Hatta Dam to glide through the serene waters aboard a kayak. Let rugged mountainscapes be the backdrop to your next excursion, with spectacular views of reddish rocky outcrops, juxtaposed with sparkling streams and local flora.

Discover the town's rich history ahead of your getaway to Hatta.
