See Parokya ni Edgar at the Coca-Cola Arena

Experience an unforgettable evening with the Filipino band, Parokya ni Edgar, at Coca-Cola Arena on 1 September. Known for their unique mix of rock novelties and satirical covers, the group has been hailed as 'National Band of the Philippines'. From alternative rock to pop and funk to rapcore, the concert will feature hits from their nearly three-decade old career.
