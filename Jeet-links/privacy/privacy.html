Introduction

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Notice (this “Privacy Notice”) governs the processing of your Personal Data by the Department of Economy and Tourism and its affiliates (collectively referred to as “DET”). It explains how we process your Personal Data and applies to the Personal Data that you provide us directly, or which we may obtain from other sources. Please read the following carefully to understand how we process your Personal Data. References to "our", "us" or "we" within this Privacy Notice are to DET.

Who we are

DET is an entity established pursuant to Law No. 20 of 2021 having its principal office at P.O. Box 594, Dubai, United Arab Emirates. DET is the principal authority for the economic development of Dubai to be the global centre for business, investment and tourism. DET is also responsible for the licensing of all commercial activity in Dubai. You can find out more about DET at: https://www.dubaidet.gov.ae/en/about-us.

Data Controller

DET acts as the Data Controller of your Personal Data. If you follow a link to a service provided by another government department, agency or other third parties, that organisation will:

  • publish and manage their own privacy notice with details of how to contact them
  • be responsible for processing any data you share with them
  • be the Data Controller
