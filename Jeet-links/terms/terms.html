Dubai Municipality Terms and Conditions - use of Dubai Desert Conservation

  1. Consider environmental, health and safety requirements, and comply with all relevant legislation.
  2. Refrain from causing damage or polluting the reserve's natural environment, and ensure adequate disposal of any waste.
  3. Ensure compliance with wildlife safety standards, taking into account wildlife movement such as birds and reptiles. Do not destroy nests, harm juvenile animals or attempt to catch them for any reason, and inform the Environment Department of Dubai Municipality of any wildlife accidents or injuries.
  4. Do not deviate from the planned route as this can damage or kill a certain plant or animal species, and may harm the ecosystem in the surrounding area.
  5. Avoid making loud sounds and maintain the area as quiet as possible. Follow the site-specific instructions stipulated on the signboards.
